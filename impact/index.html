What We Work With

The cutting-edge technology of AR & VR

Augmented Reality (AR)

Pokemon Go, IKEA, and even Snapchat filters... augmented reality is the latest craze in technology and accessible to almost everyone with a smartphone. AR allows you to view 2D & 3D objects combined with the world around you, so you can get creative!

Virtual Reality (VR)

Virtual reality transports you to another world which you can explore by walking around and interacting with the virtual environment. Also accessible by web and mobile, VR provides an immersive experience without you having to walk a single step.

Our Products

VRAL Fantasy

VRAL Fantasy generates a 3D-model or 360-camera virtual reality experience customized for your property. Upgrade from the typical boring marketing methods and give your potential customers an immersive tour!

ARtivity App

The ARtivity app is a location-based AR social media. Create augmented reality messages and objects from your own drawings, photos, or videos, and add them to your location. Discover other creations wherever you go. Interact with your friends, favorite creators, or other users around you.

ARtivity App for Businesses

Become our partner and we let us bring the magic of AR for your business! Add interactive and creative 3D models specially made for your tourism sites, event decorations, product simulations, or any other needs. All ARtivity users can instantly view official AR from our partners without having to download a new app or scan any markers.
